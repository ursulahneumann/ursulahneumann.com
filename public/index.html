Ursula Neumann PhD

Hi and welcome! I received my PhD in Cell and Developmental Biology at the University of
British Columbia researching diabetes. Currently I work as a Senior Scientist @ Pawsperity Biotechnologies,
an early stage start-up working on improving animal health through biomarker discovery.

In my free time I love to explore Vancouver’s trails, practice yoga, and take pictures of
flowers @bloomvancouver). I hope to share projects on a range of topics including just-for-fun
data analyses, easy-to-follow tutorials, and more complex machine learning explorations.
